techtuate vs. iLovePDF

Pricing and limits checked May 2026.

iLovePDF has the broadest free toolbox of the major paid services. The catch: 15 MB per file on the web, plus daily task ceilings, plus ads pushing you to Premium. techtuate has no file cap and no ads.

TL;DR

iLovePDF Free = 15 MB file cap, daily task limits, ads. iLovePDF Premium = $9/mo. techtuate = no file cap (your browser RAM is the ceiling), no ads, no sign-up, $0 forever.

What the free tier actually blocks

  • 15 MB per file on the web app. A 30-page color scan or a marketing deck export will trip this.
  • Daily task limits on free accounts.
  • Ads + Premium upsells in the interface.
  • Account required for many of the better tools.
  • Files uploaded to their servers for processing.

What techtuate does instead

  • File-size ceiling = your browser memory, typically hundreds of megabytes.
  • No task limit, no rate limit.
  • No ads, no upsell, no account.
  • Files never leave your device.

Where iLovePDF wins

  • Sheer breadth: 20+ tools across edit, convert, sign, compress.
  • Native mobile apps.
  • Office-format conversion is more polished than what a browser can do.

If you need a full-spectrum cloud PDF suite and the privacy story doesn't bother you, iLovePDF Premium is a fair $9/mo. If you mostly need the core editing operations, techtuate covers them at $0 with nothing uploaded.
