techtuate vs. pdfFiller

Pricing checked May 2026.

pdfFiller is one of the few "PDF tools" with no free tier at all - the cheapest plan is $8/mo. If your use case is filling forms a few times a year, that's $96/yr for buttons you press maybe ten times.

TL;DR

pdfFiller Basic = $8/mo (no free tier). techtuate fills PDF forms for $0, in your browser, no sign-up.

pdfFiller pricing

  • Basic: $8/mo.
  • Plus: $12/mo.
  • Premium: $15/mo.
  • Free tier: none. You have to sign up and start a trial.

What techtuate gives you free

  • Open a PDF with existing form fields.
  • Type values into the fields in a side panel.
  • Save the filled PDF.
  • Optionally "flatten" - bake the values in so fields can't be re-edited.
  • No upload, no account, no $96/yr.

Where pdfFiller actually earns its money

pdfFiller is built for heavy compliance and HR workflows: e-signature with audit trail, signer-tracking, team collaboration, integrations into Salesforce / Dropbox / etc. If you're a small business sending dozens of forms a week and need a paper trail, pdfFiller is a real product. For the "I just need to fill this one form" case, it's massive overkill.
