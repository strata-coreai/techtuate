techtuate vs. QR.io

Pricing and trial terms verified May 2026 against QR.io's public pricing page.

QR.io is a QR code platform focused on dynamic codes and campaign analytics. Their free trial gives you 7 days of premium features, then the product moves to paid plans from $7/mo. techtuate generates QR codes - including branded ones with your logo - free, in your browser, with no trial period and no account required.

TL;DR

QR.io = 7-day trial then $7/mo+ for dynamic codes, logo, and analytics. techtuate = logo, custom colors, PNG/SVG/PDF downloads, free forever, no trial, no sign-up.

Where QR.io genuinely wins

  • Dynamic QR codes with destination editing. Scan the same physical QR code but redirect users to a new URL without reprinting. Ideal for printed menus, seasonal campaigns, or any use case where the destination might change.
  • Scan tracking and analytics. QR.io records how many times each code was scanned, from where, and on which device. Useful for measuring print campaign performance.
  • Multiple code types. QR.io supports structured formats like WiFi credentials, vCards, social links, and app deep links with guided form fields. techtuate lets you paste any text and generate - you format the payload yourself.
  • Team collaboration. Their paid plans support multiple users and shared code libraries. Not relevant for individual use but useful for agencies.

Where the trial wall hits

  • 7-day trial, then paid. After a week you need to subscribe or your codes may stop working if they're dynamic.
  • Logo and custom colors require paid plan. Branding features are behind the paywall after the trial.
  • Account required from day one. Even the trial requires signing up.
  • Dynamic codes depend on QR.io's servers staying up. If you cancel or the service shuts down, any printed dynamic QR codes become dead links. Static codes printed from techtuate work forever.

What techtuate does instead

  • No trial period. All features available immediately, no account, no credit card.
  • Logo upload and color customization, free. Upload PNG, JPG, or SVG; pick any colors; download clean output.
  • Static codes that never expire. The destination is baked into the QR pixels. No server in the middle, no renewal required.
  • Download as PNG, SVG, or PDF. PDF download includes a clickable link in the banner strip.
  • Nothing stored. The browser generates the code and hands you the file. No account, no scan data, no server logs.
