About this tool

Free, forever, no sign-up. Runs entirely in your browser - nothing is uploaded. Part of techtuate, a growing collection of little tools that just work.

This generates a static QR code - the destination is baked directly into the pixels. No redirect server, no tracking, no link rot if a service shuts down.

Why logo and color customization are free

Most QR code tools (QR Tiger, Flowcode, Beaconstac) charge $5-15 per month to add your logo or change colors. The browser already has color pickers and canvas drawing built in. There is nothing to charge you for.

High error correction is automatically applied when you add a logo - this preserves scannability even with a portion of the QR obscured by the image.
