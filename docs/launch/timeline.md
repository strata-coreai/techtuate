## Rules of the day

- **Be there.** HN comments arrive in the first 2 hours. If you're not replying, the post dies in the new queue.
- **Don't pitch in replies.** Answer the question. Mention features only when directly relevant.
- **Don't share before submitting.** HN flags coordinated traffic. The HN submission is the first share; cross-posts come 2+ hours later.
- **No auto-reply, no copy-paste replies.** Each one is a few sentences in your voice. The canned bank in [hn-show-hn.md](hn-show-hn.md) is reference, not a script.
- **If it flops, that's data.** Two backup angles (image compressor launch, "$0/mo hosting" engineering post) are queued in [../launch-plan.md](../launch-plan.md). Wait 30 days, swap angle, try again.

## Bail-out conditions

Move the date if any of these are true on Monday 2026-05-25 EOD:
- PDF editor broken on mobile or in Safari.
- techtuate.com is returning a non-200 anywhere.
- You can't be at the keyboard 08:00-14:00 ET Tuesday.
- Big tech news cycle scheduled for Tuesday morning (CPI day, major Apple/Google event, etc.).

Push 1 week, not less. Half-launching is worse than waiting.
