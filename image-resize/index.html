About this tool

Free, forever, no sign-up. Runs entirely in your browser - nothing is uploaded. Part of techtuate, a growing collection of little tools that just work.

Resize by exact pixels, by a percentage, or by a target file size (say, get a photo under 500 KB for an upload limit). Animated GIFs keep every frame - the tool decodes them, scales each frame, and re-encodes a fresh optimized GIF, all locally.

Why "no upload" matters here

Most online resizers (and every "compress to X KB" site) send your image to a server. For screenshots, ID scans, or anything private, that is a real leak. Your browser already has everything needed to decode, scale, and re-encode an image, so there is nothing to send anywhere.
